PANDA

The giant panda (Ailuropoda melanoleuca) is a bear species native to China. It is easily recognizable by its distinctive black and white coloring and is known for its docile temperament and diet primarily consisting of bamboo. Pandas are considered a national treasure in China and are classified as vulnerable with efforts underway to protect their habitat and increase their population.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM?

You will find the pandas in the bamboo forest habitat near the main entrance.
